## Instructions

1. Add your **bread slices** in a hot pan. On the side, stir the **kimchi** in a bit of butter or oil.
2. After one minute, turn over one slice of bread, add the **grated cheese** on top of it. Top with the hot kimchi. Close with the other slice of bread.
3. Leave for a few minutes and turn over the entire thing for a couple more minutes.
