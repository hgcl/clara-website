## Instructions

1. Cut off head and tails of **beetroots**. Steam them until completely cooked, set the flavored water aside.
2. Blend everything until smooth, add a bit of the water if necessary. Add **tahini** and **lemon juice** to taste.
3. Optional: add thinly cut **garlic** and a dash of **olive oil** to the mix.
4. Refrigerate.
