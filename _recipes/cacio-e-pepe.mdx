## Instructions

1. Grate the **cheese** and keep aside.
2. Add the **spaghetti** in a pan and cover with boiling **water**. Mix constantly for about ten minutes, the pasta should still be slightly undercooked. The starchy water should have reduced almost completely.
3. Grind some **black pepper** into the pan, mix everything. Add the grated cheese, keep on mixing for one minute until melted.
4. Serve hot.
