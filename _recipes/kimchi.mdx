## Instructions

1. Chop the **cabbage** into bite size pieces.
1. In a large basin, soak the pieces of cabbage in cold **water**. Sprinkle **salt**.
1. Every 30 minutes, turn the cabbage over to salt evenly (total salting time will be 1.5 hours). Once done, rinse and drain thoroughly.
1. In a pot, mix **flour** with **water** and bring to a boil.
1. Add **sugar** and cook a few minutes more until translucent. Set aside to cool down.
1. Mix the cold flour porridge into a large bowl with **gochugaru**, **soy sauce**, **garlic**, **onion**, **green onion**, **leek**, **ginger** and **carrots**.
1. Add the **cabbage** to the bowl and thoroughly mix.
1. Put the kimchi into an air-tight sealed plastic container or glass jar.
1. Store in the fridge, making sure to mix it up at least once a week.
